Information Technology Consultancy Services


At Dasun Ltd, we offer a wide range of Information Technology consultancy services designed to help businesses optimize their IT operations and achieve their business objectives. Our services include:

  • IT Agency
  • Software and Hardware Supplies
  • IT Seminars and Webinars

IT Agency

  • Providing staffing solutions to meet IT talent needs, including temporary or permanent placements
  • Developing custom recruitment plans for clients to meet their unique staffing needs
  • Providing specialized consulting services to optimize IT teams and processes
  • Conducting workforce analysis and workforce planning to identify staffing gaps and opportunities for improvement
  • Providing outsourced IT services to supplement client IT teams


Software and Hardware Supplies:

  • Consulting on software and hardware selection and procurement
  • Providing vendor assessments and recommendations
  • Providing pricing and budget analysis for software and hardware procurement
  • Offering installation and configuration support
  • Providing training and support for end-users


IT Seminars and Webinars

  • Developing and delivering customized training programs for employees, managers, and executives
  • Conducting webinars and seminars on various IT topics, including cybersecurity, cloud migration, and digital transformation
  • Offering customized training programs to meet specific needs, such as software or system-specific training
  • Providing one-on-one or group coaching sessions for IT teams and individuals
  • Developing and delivering IT-focused conferences or events to bring together industry leaders and experts


At Dasun Ltd, we take a personalized approach to IT consultancy, working closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and goals. We leverage our experience and expertise to provide tailored solutions that deliver real value to our clients. Our team of highly skilled professionals is equipped with the knowledge and tools necessary to tackle even the most complex IT challenges. We are committed to delivering exceptional service to our clients and building strong and lasting relationships based on trust, integrity, and mutual respect. Contact us today to learn more about our Information Technology consultancy services and how we can help you achieve your business objectives.
