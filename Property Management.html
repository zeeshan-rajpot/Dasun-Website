Property Management

At Dasun Ltd, we offer comprehensive property management services to property owners, landlords, and tenants. Our services include:

  • Rental Services
  • Serviced Accommodation Management


Rental Services

  • Property advertising and marketing to attract tenants
  • Tenant screening, background checks, and reference verification
  • Lease preparation and negotiation
  • Rent collection and maintenance of rent records
  • Property inspections to ensure tenant compliance and identify maintenance needs
  • Coordination of property repairs and maintenance
  • 24/7 emergency response to address urgent property issues
  • Move-in and move-out inspections and handling of security deposits

Serviced Accommodation Management

  • Property advertising and marketing to attract guests
  • Booking management and reservation handling
  • Guest screening, identity verification, and reference checks
  • Cleaning and housekeeping services
  • Laundry and linen services
  • Maintenance and repair services to ensure the property is in top condition
  • 24/7 guest support and emergency response



At Dasun Ltd, we understand that property management can be complex and time-consuming, which is why we offer end-to-end solutions that are tailored to meet the unique needs of each of our clients. Our property management services are designed to help property owners and landlords maximize their return on investment and provide a hassle-free experience for their tenants. We take pride in our commitment to delivering exceptional service and building strong relationships with our clients.
