Training and development


Dasun LTD, we offer a comprehensive range of training and development services designed to help our clients improve their employees' skills and knowledge, increase productivity, and drive business results. We believe that investing in employee training and development is essential to the success of any organization, and we are committed to providing the best training and development services possible.



Our training and development services include:
  • Technical Training
  • Soft Skills Training
  • Leadership Development
  • Career Development
  • Performance Management
  • Continuing Education
  • Professional Certification

Technical Training


We offer technical training programs to help employees develop the skills they need to succeed in their roles. We provide training in software applications, equipment operation, and other relevant areas, and we customize our training programs to meet the specific needs of our clients.


Soft Skills Training


We offer soft skills training programs to help employees communicate effectively, work collaboratively, and manage their time and priorities. Our training programs cover a range of topics, including communication skills, teamwork, time management, and conflict resolution. Dasun Ltd's M&E maintenance services cover a range of mechanical and electrical systems, including heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC), elevators, plumbing, lighting, and fire safety systems. The company's skilled technicians and engineers perform routine maintenance, repairs, replacements, and upgrades as needed to ensure that these systems are functioning properly and efficiently.


Leadership Development


We offer leadership development programs to help employees who are interested in advancing into management roles. Our programs cover topics such as leadership skills, management techniques, and strategic thinking, and we provide coaching and mentoring to help participants develop their leadership abilities.


Career Development


We offer career development programs to help employees plan their career paths within their organization. Our programs include coaching and mentoring, job shadowing, cross-functional projects, and other experiences to help employees gain exposure to different areas of their organization.


Performance Management


We offer performance management services to help organizations develop effective performance management systems. We work with our clients to develop performance metrics, provide training to managers on effective performance management techniques, and provide ongoing coaching and feedback to employees.


Continuing Education


We offer support for continuing education opportunities such as professional certifications, conferences, and workshops. We provide financial and logistical support to help employees pursue their educational goals and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in their field.
