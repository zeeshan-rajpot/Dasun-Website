Procurement


Dasun LTD, we understand the importance of procurement in ensuring the success of our business and the satisfaction of our customers. We are committed to providing the highest quality products and services to our clients, and we achieve this through a strong and effective procurement process.



Our procurement process includes the following:
  • Supplier Selection
  • Contract Negotiation
  • Supplier Performance Monitoring
  • Continuous Improvement

Supplier Selection


We carefully select our suppliers based on a variety of factors, including their quality, reliability, and cost-effectiveness. We maintain strong relationships with our suppliers and work closely with them to ensure that they meet our high standards and expectations.


Contract Negotiation


We negotiate contracts with our suppliers to ensure that we receive the best possible value for our money. We work to achieve fair and transparent pricing and terms, and we carefully review and analyze all contracts before finalizing them.


Supplier Performance Monitoring


We monitor the performance of our suppliers to ensure that they continue to meet our high standards. We track delivery times, product quality, and other key metrics to ensure that our suppliers are meeting our expectations.

Continuous Improvement


: We are committed to continuous improvement in our procurement process. We regularly review our processes and procedures and look for opportunities to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance the quality of our products and services.



Dasun LTD, we take a collaborative approach to procurement, working closely with our suppliers and other stakeholders to achieve our goals. We prioritize transparency and communication in all aspects of our procurement process and are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and ethical behaviour. We believe that a strong and effective procurement process is essential to our success and the success of our clients, and we are committed to delivering the best possible products and services through our procurement efforts.
